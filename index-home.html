ABOUT

Hello, I am Janeth Aráuz “ZIOI”

I am an illustrator, born in Nicaragua with a passion for simple and careful lines. My favorite tools are charcoal, solid pen or texture brushes. I like to vary my styles and techniques such as canvases with watercolor, crayon, chalk, charcoal, pen and many more. In this small space you will be able to take a look at my work, I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoy creating it.
